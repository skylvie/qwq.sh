DMCA Takedown Policy

    


    


    

        

            
overview :

            

                qwq.sh respects copyright and complies with the
                Digital Millennium Copyright Act.
                If you are a copyright owner (or authorized to act on behalf of one) and believe that content hosted on our services infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown notice.
            

        


        

            
requirements :

            
Your notice must include all of the following:

            
    
                
  • Your electronic signature, or the signature of a person authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
    • 
                
  • A clear description of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
    • 
                
  • The specific URL(s) or sufficient information for us to locate the infringing content.
    • 
                
  • Your contact details, including at minimum an email address.
    • 
                
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the content is not authorized by the copyright owner, their agent, or the law.
    • 
                
  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
    • 
            

        


        

            
submit :

            
    
                
  • 
                    dmca@qwq.sh
                    - send requests in plain text. we review every submission and will follow up on valid notices.
