DMCA Takedowns


    


    

        
overview :

        

            qwq.sh takes copyright seriously.
            If you own a copyright (or act on behalf of someone who does) and believe that something hosted on our server infringes on it,
            you can file a takedown request with us under the DMCA.
            Your notice needs to include all of the following:
        

    


    

        
requirements :

        
    
            
  • Your electronic signature, or that of someone authorized to act on the copyright owner's behalf.
    • 
            
  • A clear description of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
    • 
            
  • The specific URL(s) or enough information for us to find the infringing content.
    • 
            
  • Your contact details, including at minimum an email address.
    • 
            
  • A statement that you genuinely believe the use of the content is not authorized by the copyright owner, their agent, or the law.
    • 
            
  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that everything in your notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on the copyright owner's behalf.
    • 
        

    


    

        
submit :

        
    
            
  • 
                dmca@qwq.sh
                - send requests as plain text. we review every submission and will follow up on valid requests.
            
    • 
        

    


    

        
notice :

        
Be aware that filing a false or misleading DMCA notice can expose you to legal liability under Section 512(f) of the DMCA.
