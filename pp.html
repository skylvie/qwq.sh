Privacy | qwq.sh

        
Privacy Policy

    


    


    

        

            
terms :

            
    
                
  • "We/Us" refers to qwq.sh administrator(s).
    • 
                
  • "Service/Services" refers to any service provided by qwq.sh.
    • 
                
  • "User" refers to any person accessing qwq.sh and its services.
    • 
            

        


        

            
overview :

            
We don't want your data and we collect as little as possible. This policy explains what we do and don't collect across our services.

        


        

            
what we collect :

            
    
                
  • We use Cloudflare for all of our services. Cloudflare's own privacy policy applies to all traffic passing through their network.
    • 
                
  • We maintain Caddy access logs for most services. These logs include your IP address, request path, timestamp, user agent, and other standard HTTP request data. These are used solely for abuse prevention and debugging.
    • 
            

        


        

            
per-service details :

            
    
                
  • qwq.sh - This is a static website. No user data is processed beyond standard access logs.
    • 
                
  • SearXNG - Zero logging. No search queries are recorded or stored. No one, including administrators, can see your searches.
    • 
                
  • PrivateBin - All pastes are end-to-end encrypted. Administrators cannot view the contents of any paste.
    • 
                
  • Kutt - No access logs are kept. The only data visible to administrators is the list of currently existing shortened URLs. No information about who created them is stored.
    • 
                
  • Arch Linux mirror - No download history is logged. The only information visible to administrators is clients currently downloading files and their IP addresses during the active connection.
    • 
                
  • x.qwq.sh - No logs of any kind are collected.
    • 
                
  • Uptime monitor - Powered by Uptime Kuma. No access logs are kept on the Uptime Kuma instance itself.
    • 
                
  • Guestbook - The guestbook is an external service hosted by Atabook and is not operated by us. Their own privacy policy applies.
    • 
            

        


        

            
what we don't do :

            
    
                
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or fingerprinting.
    • 
                
  • We do not sell, share, or provide data to any third parties.
    • 
            

        


        

            
data requests :

            
You may request an export of any data we hold about you. Contact us and we will provide what we have, if anything.
